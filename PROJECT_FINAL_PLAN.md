# 2. Business Strategy

## Business Overview

The Bangladesh-Malaysia Chamber of Commerce & Industry (BMCCI) is a bilateral business organization dedicated to strengthening economic cooperation, trade, investment, and commercial relationships between Bangladesh and Malaysia.

Traditionally, BMCCI has operated as a chamber that facilitates business networking, trade missions, investment promotion, policy advocacy, and member services. However, modern businesses expect digital-first services that are available anytime and anywhere.

The BMCCI Digital Gateway aims to transform the Chamber into a technology-driven organization capable of delivering value digitally while expanding its international reach.

---

# Business Vision

To become South Asia's leading bilateral digital chamber platform that connects businesses, governments, investors, entrepreneurs, and institutions through innovative digital services.

# 4. Member Portal

## Overview

The Member Portal is the primary digital workspace for all registered BMCCI members. It provides secure access to chamber services, business opportunities, networking, trade information, event participation, document management, and personalized recommendations.

Rather than acting as a simple account page, the Member Portal is designed to function as a complete business hub that strengthens engagement between BMCCI and its members while facilitating collaboration between businesses in Bangladesh and Malaysia.
