Help desk

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions regarding fees, approvals, and activations.

Any registered business corporation, consultancies, banks, freight shippers, or raw materials manufacturers operating in Bangladesh or Malaysia with a valid trade registration license is eligible to join.
Fees differ depending on the category selected: Corporate Patrons ($500/Year), Associate Members ($250/Year), Individual Consultants ($150/Year), and Overseas Patrons ($600/Year). A one-time registry fee applies.
Once forms files and trade license copies are uploaded online, secretariats verify company parameters within 3 business days. Board committee votes are compiled monthly, after which activation invoices are emailed.
