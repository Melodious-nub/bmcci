Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common queries regarding intelligence datasets, reports access, and compliance updates.

All statistical indicators, GDP values, and import-export charts are compiled from official customs declarations registries, central bank updates, and ministries datasets (such as BIDA, BEZA, MIDA, and MATRADE).
Indices tracking total trade volume and investment inflows are revised quarterly. Policy briefs regarding double taxation compliance changes are updated immediately following state committee ratifications.
Quarterly summaries are accessible to public visitors. Standard investment guides, specific customs handbooks, and direct B2B directory search keys are reserved exclusively for registered chamber members.
