Bilateral Trade Treaty Agreement Ratified by Ministers

July 12, 2026 Published by Secretariats

Executive Summary

State ministries in charge of trade policy have ratified the double taxation exemption treaties, paving channels for high-value logistics concessions.

Key Highlights:
  • 100% withholding tax exemptions cleared on software JVs.
  • Customs clearance procedures unified under a single-window cargo manifest.
  • Direct deep-water route berthing concessions cleared for Port Klang and Chittagong.
