Incentives Policy

FDI & Investment Incentives

Both Bangladesh and Malaysia cabinets offer robust tax, duty, and zoning concessions to encourage foreign capital allocations.

Foreign companies establishing manufacturing units in approved Economic Zones (BEZA) receive complete corporate tax exemptions for the first 5-7 years, scaling gradually to standard rates.
Tariff lists are updated to permit 100% duty exemptions on incoming heavy machinery, testing gear, and capital factory equipment imported directly for industrial JV projects.
Foreign corporations operating within designated commercial tech zones are allowed 100% profit and equity dividend repatriation allowances without local withholding taxes.
