Historical Context

A Bilateral Catalyst for Economic Synergy

Founded in 2003, the Bangladesh Malaysia Chamber of Commerce & Industry (BMCCI) serves as the primary gateway bridging government entities, trade ministries, corporate leaders, and logistics agencies across both friendly nations.

Our secretariat advocates for favorable double taxation treatments, tariff concessions, industrial zone allotments, and streamlined business visa issuance mechanisms.

Our Strategic Mission

To foster, facilitate, and expand bilateral trade, investment, joint ventures, and technology transfers between Bangladesh and Malaysia commercial entities by representing private sector interests before regulatory cabinets.

Our Strategic Vision

To establish a highly integrated, frictionless, and digitally empowered bilateral economic zone corridor that positions Bangladesh and Malaysia as core corporate hubs in South Asia and ASEAN commerce respectively.
