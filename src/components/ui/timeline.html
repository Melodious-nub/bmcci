Year 2003

Chamber Inauguration

Official establishment of the Bangladesh Malaysia Chamber of Commerce & Industry (BMCCI) in Dhaka to consolidate bilateral ties and streamline import-export licensing.

Year 2012

Deep-Water Trade Concessions

Successful negotiations facilitating prioritized shipping container berthing rights between Chittagong Port Authority and Port Klang.

Year 2025

Double Taxation Update

Submission and ratification of modernized double taxation avoidance rules for cross-border investments and intellectual property software licensing fees.
