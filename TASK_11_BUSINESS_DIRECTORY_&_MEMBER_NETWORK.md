# OBJECTIVE

Create a premium digital business directory where users can discover, explore and connect with BMCCI member companies.

The directory should become a networking ecosystem.

Users should be able to:

• Search companies

• Filter businesses

• Discover industries

• View company profiles

• Explore business opportunities

• Connect with organizations

The experience should feel like an international B2B marketplace.
