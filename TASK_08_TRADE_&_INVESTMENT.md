OBJECTIVE

Design a premium Trade & Investment page that acts as the central business gateway for investors, exporters, importers, manufacturers, SMEs, and entrepreneurs interested in Bangladesh–Malaysia trade relations.

This page should provide business opportunities, investment insights, trade sectors, market information, and government support resources in a visually engaging and executive-level interface.

The page should feel like an international investment portal rather than a standard information page.
