# OBJECTIVE

Design and develop one premium enterprise Footer System that will be reused across the entire BMCCI Digital Gateway.

The Footer is not just a collection of links.

It is the final business engagement section of every page.

Every page must use this Footer without modification.

Never redesign it later.

---

# DESIGN GOAL

The Footer should create trust and encourage users to continue engaging with BMCCI.

The Footer should feel similar to premium business platforms such as:

• Stripe
• Apple
• World Economic Forum
• Dubai Chamber
• Bloomberg

The Footer should never feel like a traditional government or chamber website.

---

The Footer consists of three major sections:

1. Business Call-To-Action
