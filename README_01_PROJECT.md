# BMCCI DIGITAL GATEWAY

## README 01 - Project Vision, Product Philosophy & Global AI Instructions

Version: 1.0

---

# PROJECT OVERVIEW

You are acting as a Senior Product Designer, UX Architect, Frontend Engineer and Creative Director.

Your responsibility is to build a premium frontend prototype for the Bangladesh Malaysia Chamber of Commerce & Industry (BMCCI).

This project is NOT a traditional Chamber of Commerce website.

This project is NOT a government portal.

This project is NOT a corporate company profile.

The goal is to create a modern international digital platform that represents trade, investment, innovation, diplomacy, tourism and business collaboration between Bangladesh and Malaysia.

The final prototype should look like a premium international business platform suitable for presentation to government officials, investors, foreign delegations and chamber members.

The website should immediately communicate trust, professionalism and international collaboration.

---

# PRIMARY OBJECTIVES

The platform should help visitors:

• Understand BMCCI's mission and vision.

• Discover business and investment opportunities.

• Register for business events.

• Explore economic insights.

• Find chamber members.

• Learn about Bangladesh and Malaysia.

• Apply for membership.

• Contact BMCCI.

Every page must guide users toward meaningful business engagement.
