# SECTION 01 — HERO

Height: 90–100vh.

Layout: Two Columns.

Left (45%)

- Small eyebrow label:
  "Bangladesh Malaysia Chamber of Commerce & Industry"

- Large headline (2–3 lines max):
  "Connecting Businesses. Empowering Growth. Bridging Nations."

- Supporting paragraph (max 3 lines).

- Two CTA buttons:
  - Become a Member
  - Explore Opportunities

- Quick stats row:
  • 650+ Members
  • 120+ Events
  • $250M+ Trade Facilitated
  • 2 Nations Connected

Right (55%)

Large editorial image of a business meeting or Dhaka–Kuala Lumpur skyline.

Overlay 3 floating glass cards:

- Upcoming Event
- Trade Opportunity
- Live GDP Widget

Background

Very subtle radial gradient.

Very light world map pattern at 5% opacity.

Animation

Headline reveals line-by-line.

Buttons fade.

Cards float slowly.

Hero image has subtle parallax.
